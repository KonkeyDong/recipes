# Pumpkin Bread

Makes two loaves of pumpkin bread.

## Ingredients

* **2 Cups** white sugar
* **1 Cup** vegetable oil
* **3 Eggs**
* **2 Cups** pumpkin puree
* **3 Cups** sifted all-purpose flour
* **1 Tsp.** Baking soda
* **3/4 Tsp.** Baking powder
* **1 Tsp.** Cinnamon
* **1/2 Tsp.** Nutmeg
* **1/2 Tsp.** Cloves
* **3/4 Tsp.** Salt

## Recipe

1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
1. Blend **sugar** and **vegetable oil** with electric mixer or hand mixer.
1. Beat in **eggs**, one at a time; add **pumpkin** and blend.
1. Sift **flour**, **baking powder**, **baking soda**, **cinnamon**, **nutmeg**, **cloves**, and **salt** into creamed mixture.
1. Beat at low speed until blended.
1. Pour batter into **_two_ 9" X 5" X 2.5"** loaf pans.
1. Bake in oven for **1 hour** or until slightly browned.
1. After baking, **leave in pan for 15 minutes** to cool on a wire rack.
1. Remove from pans; cool completely on wire rack.
1. (optional) Sprinkle with powder sugar.
